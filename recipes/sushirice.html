Sushi Rice

Creating sushi rice, can be quick and easy!

Starting with a bag of Jasmine rice, rinse 2 cups of uncooked rice untill the water is no longer cloudy. Then add water to the rice in a 1:2 ratio of rice to water. Then place the rice/water mixture on high just until it boils then reduce the heat to low for 8 to 10 mins.

Once rice is cooked, then mix in the additional ingredients and let sit for 15 to 20 mins.

  • 2 c. cooked rice
  • 2TBS sugar
  • 1/4 c. rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. cook the rice
  2. combine ingredients with rice while hot.
  3. let rice cool for 15 mins, then use
