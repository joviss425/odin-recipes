Taiyaki

Taiyaki are yummy fish shapped pasteries from Japan.

These fish shapped treats are not fishy but are filled with tasty chocolate, sweet red bean paste and creams.

  • Taiyaki pan
  • Pancake batter
  • Filling of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat the Taiyaki pan
  2. Put a little batter on to both sides.
  3. Put a little filling in the middle of both sides and top with a little more batter.
  4. Cook for 3 mins on both sides on medium low
