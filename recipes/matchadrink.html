Matcha drink

Matcha is finely ground green tea leaves. Instead of just steeping the leaves you injest the powdered form of the leaf in your drink

Matcha green tea does have caffine but it is a slow release caffine that can keep you alert longer

  • 8 oz of warm water
  • 1 tsp matcha
  • sweetner of choice

Steps

  1. heat the water to 175 degrees
  2. Add Matcha to water
  3. Enjoy!
